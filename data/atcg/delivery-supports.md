# Delivery Supports
Delivery is a broadly enabling support to cover delivering equipment, setting up equipment and/or training the participant to use the equipment as a one-off transaction that is linked to the primary support. Providers must use the appropriate support item according to their registration group when preparing quotes or invoices for review by the delegate where required. Providers must first claim the primary support item (listed on the invoice or referred to in the participant’s plan) when lodging a payment request before (or at the same time) as claiming a delivery cost using one of the below codes.
These support items are not subject to price limits. See page 12 for further information on how to claim for support items that are not subject to a price limit.
